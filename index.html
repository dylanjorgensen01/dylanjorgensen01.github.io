About Me

I am currently a senior at the University of South Carolina majoring in Biological Sciences and minoring in Criminal Justice and Psychology. At the University of South Carolina, I have been involved in Tri Beta, Phi Kappa Tau, and Capstone scholars. Tri Beta is a national honors society for biology majors and have been able to make multiple connections with professionals in the biology field. Within Phi Kappa Tau, I have helped organize community service events and have been able to make lifelong connections. Capstone Scholars has also allowed me to participate in community service and grow as a young professional throughout my college career.

Outside of school, I am the middle of 3 children from Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. When I am at home I enjoy multiple hobbies including: snowboarding, camping, and playing sports. Once a year my whole extended family goes on a camping trip to Salt Fork, Ohio. At the campgrounds, I enjoy water skiing, tubing, hiking, and spending time with my family. In addition, I love to go out and play either rugby or spikeball with friends whenever there is nice weather.
